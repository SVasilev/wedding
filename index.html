The Netherlands

Our first time traveling together, in the summer of 2019, we embarked on an adventure to The Netherlands. Amongst the flowers, windmills, and canals, the two of us fell in love. Since then, we lived in historic Delft, learned some Dutch, made lots of great friends, and knew we had to marry in the place so close to our hearts.

The Netherlands hosts one major international airport, Schiphol, which receives four direct flights from Atlanta daily. If you're flying to The Netherlands from another European city, you might also consider Eindhoven Airport (RyanAir) or Rotterdam The Hague Airport.

Local Travel: Dutch public transit leads the world. If you can ride a bike, a rental car is not necessary.
